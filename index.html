Exploring the World of Cyclistic using SQL

The Cyclistic data analysis project aims to understand the differences in service usage between annual members and casual riders. The goal is to identify trends and patterns that could help target casual riders in marketing campaigns. The project uses data from Motivate International Inc., covering last year's usage of Cyclistic. After importing the data into SSMS, The trip duration was calculated, and the trips were categorized as weekdays or weekends. The data was then analyzed to find out the average trip duration and number of rides for annual members and casual riders on weekdays and weekends. The analysis revealed that a higher percentage of casual riders use the service on weekends compared to members. Overall, the project provides valuable insights that can inform targeted marketing strategies to increase ridership among casual riders.
